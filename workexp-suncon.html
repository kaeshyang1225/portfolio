My internship life at Sunway Construction Group

November 2019 ~ February 2020 (3 months)

Sunway Velocity Phase Two is sited at Jalan Peel, Kuala Lumpur. The project is a high-rise residential development consisting of two residential towers (1051 residential units) and one office building. It is an extension of the existing Sunway Velocity shopping mall development located next to the project site. It sits at a strategical location where the tenants will be benefited from the direct access to amenities such as the shopping mall, medical centre, college, and a central park within walking distance, not to mention a safe connection to public transport such as the existing MRT/LRT stations via linking bridges.

When I was working on this project, I had been through and supervised the transfer slab construction for Residential Tower B and Tower C, with typical apartment floor construction from level 9 onwards, and also Office Tower C construction from level 6 onwards.

Impacts

  • Early Exposure in the field of Civil Engineering
  • Importance of being a team player
  • Critical thinking and analytical skills
